Project Operations

Onward Gaming Republic

Community operations and engagement strategies for a 5,000+ member gaming community

Community Manager 5,000+ Members Ongoing
Visit OGR Server

Context & Challenges

Served as Community Manager for Onward Gaming Republic, responsible for overall community health, engagement strategies, and member retention. The community required operational improvements in participation, retention, dispute management, and scalable processes.

Actions Taken

  • Engagement Programs: Launched weekly events, tournaments, and community showcases to increase active participation
  • Retention Initiatives: Implemented welcome flows, member check-ins, and milestone celebrations to improve stickiness
  • Dispute Resolution: Created structured processes for handling player conflicts, team disputes, and moderation appeals
  • Moderation Framework: Developed clear community guidelines and trained moderation team on consistent enforcement
  • Feedback Loops: Established regular surveys and town halls to gather member input on community direction
  • Cross-Promotion: Coordinated with partner communities for collaborative events and member growth
  • Content Strategy: Created announcement templates and content calendar for consistent communication

Impact

Achieved healthier community operations with sustained growth. Members reported higher satisfaction with event programming and dispute handling. The moderation team operated more efficiently with clear guidelines, and leadership had better visibility into community trends through regular reporting.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Community Operations: Managed day-to-day operations for 5,000+ member gaming community
  • Engagement Strategy: Designed and executed programs to increase active participation
  • Conflict Resolution: Mediated disputes and maintained community health
  • Team Leadership: Trained and coordinated moderation team for consistent enforcement
  • Stakeholder Communication: Provided regular reporting to leadership on community trends
  • Process Design: Created scalable workflows for moderation, events, and member onboarding
