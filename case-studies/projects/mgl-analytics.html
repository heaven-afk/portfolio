Strategic Insights

Growth Trajectory

Consistent 25% growth over 4 months confirms positive operational health. Peak of 3,008 members on Jan 2, 2026 demonstrates successful promotional events and content releases.

Brand-Led Acquisition

80-96% of joins via custom vanity URL indicates strong brand recognition and marketing effectiveness. This concentration highlights importance of brand protection and invite diversification.

Engagement Quality

Peak 13.4% monthly communication rate (November 2025, 1,594 visitors) exceeds typical community standards, demonstrating strong member participation and content quality.

Retention Performance

42.7% 30-day retention in November (398 new members → 170 retained) validates onboarding automation and community structure investments.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Analytics Tracking: Leveraged Discord native tools for growth, engagement, acquisition, and retention metrics
  • Data Interpretation: Converted raw metrics into actionable operational insights
  • Acquisition Strategy: Validated vanity URL-led brand approach with 80-96% attribution
  • Retention Focus: Achieved above-average 42.7% stickiness through onboarding optimization
  • Performance Reporting: Clear visualization of community health for stakeholders
