Ranking & Performance Analytics Systems

Esports & Community Competitions | Systems Design

Context

Developed ranking and scoring systems for esports and community competitions, along with operational reporting frameworks. The goal was to provide consistent, transparent performance tracking and decision support for tournament organizers and moderation teams.

Problem

Organizations faced challenges with:

  • Inconsistent performance tracking across events
  • Lack of transparency in ranking calculations
  • Difficulty in making fair, data-backed decisions
  • No standardized reporting cadence for stakeholders

Actions Taken

  • Metric Definition: Identified and documented key performance indicators relevant to competitions
  • Scoring Logic: Designed clear, auditable scoring formulas with weighted factors
  • Reporting Cadence: Established regular reporting schedules for teams and moderators
  • Documentation: Created comprehensive guides explaining methodology for transparency
  • Fairness & Compliance: Built in checks for edge cases and dispute resolution paths

Data & Metrics

  • Metrics tracked: Match results, placement points, participation rates, consistency scores
  • Methodology: Weighted scoring with decay factors and tier multipliers
  • Reporting: Weekly summaries for teams, monthly reports for leadership

Outcome

The systems enabled clearer decision-making with consistent, explainable rankings. Teams and participants could understand how scores were calculated, reducing disputes. Moderators had defined escalation paths for edge cases, and leadership received regular insights into competitive health and trends.

Key Skills Demonstrated

  • Data Analytics
  • Metric Design
  • Documentation
  • Governance
