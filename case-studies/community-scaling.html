Community Scaling & Retention

Onward Gaming Republic | Community Manager

Context

Served as Community Manager for Onward Gaming Republic, a gaming community with over 5,000 members. Responsible for overall community health, engagement strategies, and member retention.

Problem

The community required attention in several key areas:

  • Increase overall participation and daily active engagement
  • Improve member retention over time
  • Manage disputes fairly and consistently
  • Scale operations while maintaining community quality

Actions Taken

  • Moderation Framework: Implemented consistent moderation policies and trained the mod team
  • Community Initiatives: Organized tournaments, events, and content creation opportunities
  • Retention Strategies: Developed member engagement programs and recognition systems
  • Dispute Resolution: Established fair and transparent processes for handling conflicts
  • Stakeholder Communication: Regular reporting to leadership on community health metrics

Data & Metrics

  • 30% increase in community participation
  • 15% year-over-year increase in active members
  • 5,000+ members managed across community operations

Outcome

Achieved healthier community operations with sustained growth. Members reported higher satisfaction with event programming and dispute handling. The moderation team operated more efficiently with clear guidelines, and leadership had better visibility into community trends through regular reporting.

Key Skills Demonstrated

  • Community Operations
  • Policy Enforcement
  • Stakeholder Communication
  • Analytics Reporting
