Major Gaming League

Discord Restructure, Bot Integration & Automated Workflows

Role: Discord Server Builder & Administrator | Timeframe: Sep 2025 – Jan 2026

Context

Engaged as a Discord Server Builder and Administrator for Major Gaming League, tasked with revamping their community Discord server to improve member experience and operational efficiency.

Problem & Scope

The existing Discord server faced several operational challenges:

  • Low engagement and unclear server structure
  • Inefficient onboarding process for new members
  • Manual content distribution causing delays
  • Lack of defined moderator workflows

Actions Taken

  • Server Restructure: Redesigned channel organization and role hierarchy for clarity
  • Moderator Operations: Established clear workflows and escalation procedures for the mod team
  • Bot Integrations: Implemented and configured bots for moderation, welcome messages, and utility functions
  • Automated Onboarding: Created automated welcome sequences and role assignment flows
  • Content Workflows: Set up automated content distribution to relevant channels

Server Structure & Information Architecture (Post-Implementation)

The restructured channel architecture prioritizes clarity, reduces cognitive load, and supports scalable operations. Categories are organized by function (core navigation, onboarding, news/events, support) to minimize noise and streamline moderation workflows.

Channel Categories & Organization

Channel categories (HOME, ONBOARDING, NEWS & EVENTS) with clear separation. Post-implementation production structure.

Server Guide integration showing resources, role descriptions, and onboarding path. Post-implementation production structure.

Information Architecture Principles

  • Read-only vs. Interactive Separation: Announcements and updates are kept in read-only channels to reduce noise; interactive channels are clearly labeled to encourage participation.
  • Onboarding as a Distinct Category: Dedicated ONBOARDING category ensures new members can find server guide, welcome messages, and role assignment in one place.
  • Staff-Public Boundary: Clear visual and permission-based separation between public-facing channels and staff-only operations (moderation, support, files).
  • Moderation Efficiency: Structured categories allow moderators to quickly identify context (e.g., support tickets vs. community discussions) and apply appropriate workflows.

View Full Onboarding Walkthrough

Key Automation Features

  • Dynamic Role Assignment: Member responses automatically trigger role grants (e.g., region-specific roles, game mode preferences) without manual intervention.
  • Progressive Disclosure: Channels unlock based on completed onboarding steps, reducing initial overwhelm and guiding members through activation.
  • Consistent Experience: Standardized questions and flows ensure every member receives the same high-quality onboarding, regardless of join time.
  • Reduced Moderation Load: Automated verification and role assignment free moderators to focus on community engagement and edge cases.

Outcome & Impact

The revamped server delivered measurable operational improvements across automation, structure, and analytics-driven decision-making:

  • Scalable Workflows: Automation reduced manual content distribution and onboarding tasks by ~25%.
  • Improved Member Experience: Clearer navigation and structured onboarding contributed to 40% increase in engagement signals.
  • Data-Informed Operations: Analytics infrastructure provided visibility into acquisition sources, churn patterns, and activation friction.
  • Clearer Moderation Processes: Defined escalation paths and bot-assisted workflows improved team efficiency and response times.

Post-implementation, the server supports 3,000+ members with a streamlined operational model and evidence-based optimization approach.
